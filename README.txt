Tide & Shell Website

Files included:
- index.html
- styles.css
- script.js
- assets/moodboard.jpeg

How to use:
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open index.html in your browser.
3. Edit the wording, brand name, prices and Formspree link inside index.html.
4. Replace assets/moodboard.jpeg with your own product/lifestyle photos when ready.

To change the brand name:
Search for "Tide & Shell" in index.html and replace it with your chosen name.
